FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How many currencies does Divix support? ▼
Divix supports more than 150 world currencies, including all major reserve currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CNY) as well as many emerging-market and exotic currencies.
How often are exchange rates updated? ▼
Rates refresh continuously throughout the day from trusted financial data providers. When you open the app, you always see the latest available rate; the timestamp is shown next to each pair.
Does Divix work without an internet connection? ▼
Yes. The last fetched rates are cached on your device, so you can keep converting even when you're offline — useful when traveling abroad with limited connectivity.
Does Divix charge any conversion fees? ▼
No. Divix is a calculator that displays mid-market rates. We do not exchange money ourselves and do not add any fee or markup. Final amounts at your bank may differ due to bank spreads and fees.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No. Divix doesn't require any sign-up. Open the app and start converting — no email, no password, no personal data.
Is it really completely free? ▼
Yes, 100% free. All features are available from the moment you download the app. The only optional purchase is removing ads — a one-time payment of $2.99, no subscriptions.
